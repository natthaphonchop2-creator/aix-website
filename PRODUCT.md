# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

AiX Club serves Thai business owners, operators, creators, and working teams who want to keep using AI in practical work without tracking every new tool by themselves. They arrive with business goals rather than technical goals: they want clearer workflows, better content, faster admin work, and a place to keep learning as AI changes.

The homepage visitor should feel that AiX is a continuing member platform, not a one-time course shop. Their main job is to understand the yearly membership, see what they can keep learning inside the platform, and enter the member experience with confidence.

## Product Purpose

AiX Club is a learning platform for practical business AI. Members pay one yearly price, 1,999 THB, and get ongoing access to updated lessons, live or replay content, resources, tools, and learning paths as the AI landscape changes.

Success for the homepage means a visitor understands three things quickly:

- AiX keeps updating the learning material so members do not have to worry about falling behind.
- Membership is not limited to one course. Members can continue into related topics under the same yearly price.
- The platform is approachable for non-technical business users while still being credible enough for real work.

## Brand Personality

Always updated, calm confidence, practical learning.

The voice should be friendly, direct, and business-useful. It should avoid sounding like a hard-sell course advertisement or a technical developer tool. AiX should feel like a reliable learning companion that keeps the member current.

## Anti-references

Avoid course-storefront layouts that lead with discount urgency, isolated course cards, fake scarcity, or sales-page pressure. Avoid overly technical AI dashboards, code-heavy visuals, terminal aesthetics, and jargon-first explanations. Avoid generic SaaS landing-page patterns that make the platform feel interchangeable.

The homepage should not look like a catalog of disconnected classes. It should feel like a guided learning platform with a clear membership promise.

## Design Principles

- Lead with continuity: show that members keep learning as AI changes.
- Make membership the product: position 1,999 THB per year as platform access, not a single-course price.
- Explain through everyday work: connect AI learning to business tasks, team workflows, content, sales, admin, and decision-making.
- Keep the experience human: use Thai-first copy, accessible language, and clear next steps.
- Show the platform, not just the pitch: preview paths, resources, updates, and member spaces as parts of one learning system.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, keyboard access, and readable Thai typography. Support reduced motion. Avoid interactions that depend only on hover. Keep Thai copy scannable for non-technical users and make pricing, membership scope, and calls to action explicit.
